Overview of Hedge Funds

Created for Alchemists Academy by MacLane Wilkison

Common Characteristics

  1. Flexibility in investments (long and short)
  2. Ability to borrow money to enhance returns
  3. Minimal regulation
  4. Relatively illiquid
  5. Investors include only HNWI and institutions
  6. Performance-based fee structure
  7. Absolute return orientation

Leverage

Often increase leverage using marginal loans, repurchase agreements, short sales, and off-balance sheet products

Drivers of Recent Growth

  • Diversification
  • Absolute returns
  • Increased institutional investing
  • Favorable market environment
  • Human capital growth
  • Financial innovation
