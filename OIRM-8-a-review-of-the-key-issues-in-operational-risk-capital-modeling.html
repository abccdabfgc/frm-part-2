Loss Distribution Approach (LDA)

  • Couples the severity distribution of loss form a single event with a frequency distribution of events over a given horizon to arrive at the aggregate loss distribution for a given type of event over the horizon

Dataset Issues

  • Internal data
    • Classification
    • Length of sample
    • Frequency
    • Severity
    • Delays in reporting
    • Protracted/split events
    • Mergers & acquisitions
  • External data
    • Relevance of data points
    • Quantity of data points
    • Nature of the information in an external database
    • Reporting bias
    • Scale bias

Frequency and Severity Distributions
