Portfolio Risk: Analytical Methods

Created for Alchemists Academy by MacLane Wilkison

Portfolio VaR

  • Diversified VaR - a portfolio VaR that accounts for diversification
  • Individual VaR - the VaR of one component taken in isolation
  • Undiversified VaR - the sum of individual VaRs (portfolio VaR) when there is no short position and all correlations are unity

VaR Tools

  • Marginal VaR - change in portfolio VaR resulting from taking an additional dollar of exposure to a given component
  • Incremental VaR - change in VaR owing to a new position
  • Best hedge - additional amount to invest in an asset in order to minimize the risk of the overall portfolio
  • Component VaR - a partition of portfolio VaR that indicates how much the portfolio VaR would change if a given component was removed
