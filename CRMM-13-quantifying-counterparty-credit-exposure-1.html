Quantifying Credit Exposure

  • Mark-to-market + add-ons
  • Semi-analytical methods
  • Monte Carlo simulation
    1. Select factors
    2. Generate scenarios
    3. Revalue individual positions
    4. Aggregate exposures for each netting set
    5. Account for effects of collateral
    6. Extract desired metrics
MtM + add-ons approximates the future exposure by taking the current exposure and adding a component that represents the uncertainty of the PFE in the future. Semi-analytical models requires making an assumption regarding the risk factor(s) driving the exposure, finding the distribution of the exposure as defined by said risk factor(s), and calculating a semi-analytical approximation to a risk metric for that distribution

Modelling Netting

  • Netting must be incorporated before calculating quantities such as EE
  • Netting factor = EPE (netting) / EPE (no netting)
