Portfolio Performance Evaluation

Conventional Performance Evaluation

  • Time-weighted and dollar-weighted returns
  • Sharpe measure - divides average portfolio excess return by standard deviation of returns
  • Terynor measure - gives excess return per unit of systematic risk
  • Jensen meaure - average return on portfolio over and above CAPM prediction
  • Information ratio - divides alpha of portfolio by nonsystematic risk
  • M2 = rP* - rM

Performance Measurement for Hedge Funds

  • Risk profiles can change rapidly
  • Tend to invest in illiquid assets, thus liquidity premiums may obfuscate true alpha
  • May expose fund to infrequent but severe losses
  • Turnover can lead to survivorship bias

Style Analysis

Definition: Regresses a fund's returns on indices representating a range of asset classes. The regression coefficient on each index measures the fund's implicit allocation to that "style"

Morningstar's Risk-Adjusted Rating (RAR)

Definition: A measured based on a comparison of funds to a peer group (selected on teh basis of investment universe and portfolio characteristics). Risk-adjusted performance is ranked across funds within a style group
