Cash Collateralized Debt Obligations

Created for Alchemists Academy by MacLane Wilkison

Introduction

Definition: An asset-backed security structure in which the securitized products issued have principal and interest backed by the cash flows on a pool of debt instrument collateral

Types of CDOs

  • By type of collateral:
    • Collateralized loan obligation (CLO)
    • Collateralized bond obligation (CBO)
    • Structured finance CDO
  • By economic motivations of issuer:
    • Balance sheet CDO
    • Arbitrage CDO

Balance Sheet CDOs

  • Motivations and benefits:
    • Customized credit risk transfer
    • Monetization of assets
    • Reducing adverse selection costs
    • Funded credit protection
    • Regulatory capital arbitrage
  • Rating considerations:
    • Collateral requirements and asset quality tests
    • Coverage tests
