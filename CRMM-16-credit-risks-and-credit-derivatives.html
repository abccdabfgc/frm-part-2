Credit Risks as Options

  • ST = Max(VT-F, 0)
  • DT = F - Max(F-VT, 0)
ST = value of equity at date T. VT = value of firm at date T. F = face value of debt. DT = market value of debt at time T.

  • CreditRisk+
  • CreditMetrics
  • KMV Model

Credit Derivatives

Definition: Hedging instruments for credit risks

  • Credit events include (1) failure to make a required payment, (2) restructuring that makes any creditor worse off, (3) invocation of a cross-default clause, and (4) bankruptcy
  • Credit derivatives include puts, swap contracts, credit default swaps, total return swaps, and futures contracts
