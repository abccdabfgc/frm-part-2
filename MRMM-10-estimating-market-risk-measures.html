Estimating Market Risk Measures

Created for Alchemists Academy by MacLane Wilkison

Data

  • Profit/loss data: P/Lt = Pt+Dt-Pt-1
  • Loss/profit data: -P/Lt
  • Arithmetic return data: rt = (Pt+Dt)/Pt-1 - 1
  • Geometric return data: Rt = ln[(Pt+Dt)/Pt-1]

Estimating Historical Simulation VaR

This approach estimates VaR by ordered loss historical observations

Estimating Parametric VaR

  • Normally distributed profits/losses: αVaR = -μP/L+σP/Lzα
  • Normally distributed arithmetic returns: αVaR = -(μr-σrzα)Pt-1
  • Lognormal VaR: αVaR = Pt-1-P* = Pt-1(1-epx[μR-σRzα])
'zα' = the standard normal variate corresponding to α.

Estimating Coherent Risk Measures

  • Expected shortfall - the probability-weighted average of tail losses
