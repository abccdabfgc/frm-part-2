Roll's Critique

  1. Only one testable hypothesis relating to CAPM: the market portfolio is mean-variance efficient
  2. All other implications of the model follow this assumption and, therefore, are not independently testable
  3. If betas are calculated against ex-post mean-variance efficient portfolios, they will satisfy the SML relation regardless
  4. CAPM is not testable unless all individual assets are included in the sample
  5. A proxy for the market portfolio might be mean-variance efficient even when the true market isn't and most reasonable proxies will be highly correlated with eachother regardless of mean-variance-efficiency
