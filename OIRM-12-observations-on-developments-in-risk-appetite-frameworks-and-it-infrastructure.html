Observations on Developments in Risk Appetite Frameworks and IT Infrastructure

Created for Alchemists Academy by MacLane Wilkison

Implementing a Risk Appetite Framework (RAF)

  1. Necessitates strong internal relationships
  2. Establish strong accountabiity structures to ensure clear incentives and constraints for business lines
  3. Develop a common risk appetite languages across the firm

Implementing a Risk Data Infrastructure

  1. Requires clear governance process
  2. Requires highly automated processes
  3. Requires rapid integration of disparate IT systems
